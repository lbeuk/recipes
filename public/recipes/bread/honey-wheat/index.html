Honey Wheat Bread

Ingredients

  • All Autolyse Mix
  • All Yeast Sponge
  • 1T Salt
  • 1/2C Honey
  • 1/2C EV Olive Oil
  • 100g Hard white spring wheat flour, whole grain
  • Active: 20m0s
  • Inactive: 2h0m0s
  • Total: 2h20m0s

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients (except the dusting flour) by forming your hand into a flat hook shape and mixing. Mix until dough is firm enough to knead on counter.
  2. Cover an area on your work surface well with some of the dusting flour. Don't use it all immediately, more will be put down as the dough ball picks up and incorperated the flour.
  3. Knead the dough ball using a stretch and fold method for roughly 10 minutes. The ending dough ball should be able to be stretched very thin, although not quite forming a window pane as per the nature of whole wheat flour. Cover the ball in flour as it starts sticking to your hands.
  4. Oil the outside of the dough ball and place in a covered bowl to rise for an hour, or until dough has roughly doubled in volume.
  5. Punch the dough ball down, and split into 2-3 equal portions, form into loafs by pulling the dough ball and tucking under. Place in well oiled loaf pans, and let rise for another 30 minutes.
  6. In the meantime, start preheating the oven to 350 degrees F.
  7. Place the pans in the oven, bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the tops are lightly golden and sound hollow when tapped. - > Combine all ingredients (except the dusting flour) by forming your hand into a flat hook shape and mixing. Mix until dough is firm enough to knead on counter.
